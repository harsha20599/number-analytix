What is number Analytix

Number Analytix is a free service made to make the basic analysis of a given number. This service calculates whether the number is

  • Pallindrome
  • Prime Number
  • Even or Odd
  • In a fibonacci series

Why its made

This service is made for a purpose of practice , on the basis of learning the javascript.
